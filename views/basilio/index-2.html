BACTERIAL ASSEMBLAGES
AND IDENTITY LIBRARY ONLINE

BASILIO is a browser for Microbiota across different and supports research in Microbiology, Taxonomy and Genomics. BASILIO contains listing of microorganisms found in the gut and feces of animals identifiedthru culture-dependent and culture-indepedent techniques.
With BASILIO access for researchers related to fecal microbiome, their roles in the host system and their potential application can be accessed. Web tools included in this site offer versatility and quick access to DNA sequences, and phenotypic and chemotaxonomic information. Data on fecal microbiota are obtained from published journals accessible online (NCBI PUBMED, ELSEVIER, SCIENCE DIRECT).
